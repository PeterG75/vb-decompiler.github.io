Well, no one here has said (I hope) that our data is 100% complete, or even 100% correct. I know that some of it is NOT, but you must remember that the data given here was ReverseEngineered, and also done over a long period of time; as we learned more, we got smarter...as we got smarter, we learned more. But, somethings we never went into great detail on, and some things we just never got back to updating, and some things were just not as significant as others. For example, I don't thing you will find a large amount of data on the opcodes and properties of controls; mostly because it is (relatively) easy to find this info yourself by experimenting with the VB IDE. A couple of items, including my own, are wrong because of typos..the opcodes for Set and Get LastError are reversed, for example. But, you must realize that, over time, the intent (at least mine, anyway) was not to provide every last detail on everything, but just give enough info to make it interesting enough for others to carry on. It's kind of hard to determine what is interesting to the most people and therefore what to display, vs just "giving the store away" to those who want the answers without the work (fun, I say, not work!!) of finding out.

There used to exist, someplace, the files CommonApp (and maybe was it CommonApp2?) that was used as a common target program for our disection efforts. I don't know where they are now. And, speaking (elsewhere) of Skeleton, maybe that proggie should be available here again, also.

Hmm..what parts are you speaking of, that we have left out? It may be that it is something that you should be able to determine yourself, or it may be that we never got back to it.

Anyway, keep on learning!!
