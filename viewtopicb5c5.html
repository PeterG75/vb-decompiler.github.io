I am almost finished working on a Web Service for the online structs database.

online/ws/server.php will give you info on the web service and the actual path of the service definition is:

online/ws/server.php?wsdl

Anyways in vb.net here is some code that will load the vb header and its properties into a string.

[code=text:s1kru8f2]
  1.  
  2.         Dim s As String
  3.         Dim vb As New com.vb_decompiler.VBData
  4.         Dim struct As com.vb_decompiler.struct
  5.         Dim field As com.vb_decompiler.field
  6.         Dim structId as Long = 1 'VB Header
  7.  
  8.         struct = vb.getStruct(structId)
  9.         s = "Begin " & struct.name & vbCrLf
  10.  
  11.         For Each field In vb.getFields(structId)
  12.             s &= vbTab & field.name & " As " & field.datatype & vbCrLf
  13.         Next
  14.         s &= "End"
  15.  
  16.  
  17.
